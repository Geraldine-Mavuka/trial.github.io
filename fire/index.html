About Us

Company Overview

Aero Drone Soccer(ADS) is an innovative sports and technology company based in Blantyre, Malawi. ADS pioneers in introducing the thrilling sport of drone soccer to Malawi and Southern Africa, fostering a new wave of technological enthusiasm and sporting culture. The company organizes drone soccer tournaments, offers training programs, and sells drone soccer kits.

Mission Statement

To revolutionize the sports industry in Malawi by integrating technology and entertainment, fostering a community of tech-savvy sports enthusiasts, and promoting STEM education among the youth.

Vision

To become a leading force in the sports technology industry in Africa, expanding drone soccer to every corner of the continent and making it a staple in both recreational and educational sectors.
